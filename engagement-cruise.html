Magic at Sea, our first Cruise

The MSC Meraviglia

Here's where our love of cruising began. We got engaged on this ship. Life was changed for us forever after being aboard the MSC Meraviglia.

We initially left out of Miami to head to Mexico and the Bahamas!

Our first destination was Cozumel, Mexico!

We got to experience swimming with dolphins and enjoy this lovely island!

Next we got to explore the beautiful port area for Costa Maya, Mexico.
